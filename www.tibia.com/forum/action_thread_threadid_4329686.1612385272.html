Title: Achievements for Mounts Available in Game
Abstract/Vision Statement: Player will get 1-3 achievement points for getting every single mount available in game.
Detailed Description:
  • Current situation/problem: Players get 3-6 achievements points for gaining any full outfit, this is understandable, outfits cost much money and time. What about mounts? For example Midnight Panther? I have lost about month and half a million gold pieces to catch it. Now I am proud of it, however as I am "achievement hunter" I feel a bit unsatisfied. Moreover look at Shock Head, people spent millions gold pieces for taming it, I am sure some of them want to inform on their profile on Tibia.com they have achievement for tamed it, analogously to people with Ferumbras Hat, they set on their profile achievement "Alumni" .
  • Solution: There is my proposal for achievements points and achievements titles.

    Considered things in the allocatiing points:
    1. taming item availability (e. g. Four-Leaf Clover is impossible to buy on some Open-PvP servers, because nobody has it),
    2. taming item cost,
    3. regular/irregular spawn of monster (e. g. Undead Cavebear, Midnight Panther are very rare-spawned monsters),
    4. the strenght of monster,
    5. other achievements gained by finishing related quests (e. g. The Gravedigger of Drefia Quest - 3 points, The Lion's Rock Quest - 1 point, so I assigned for The Hellgrip Mount and The Noble Lion Mount only 1 point).
