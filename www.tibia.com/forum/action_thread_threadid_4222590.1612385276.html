Abstract/Vision Statement:
Im sure older players remember the good days of making your own runes and selling them, which was kinda ruined by that you can buy them in shop. I was just thinking about this so if this has already been proposed im sorry in advance.

Detailed Description:
Current situation: Almost no one makes his own runes anymore, simply buying them in shop.
Solution: Make runes made by players deal up to 10% more damage(% can be adjusted ofc)/ or make runes from the shop do less damage.
The runes wouldnt need a special name, it would look like this:

Avalanche made by you:
You see an avalanche rune ("adori mas frigo" ). It can only be used by players with level 30 and magic level 4 or higher. It was handmade with extra power.
It weighs 0.52 oz.

Avalanche bought in shop:
You see an avalanche rune ("adori mas frigo" ). It can only be used by players with level 30 and magic level 4 or higher. It was fabricated in the Edron Manafactory.
It weighs 0.52 oz.

Perhaps it would be nice if they share the same hotkey, if you decide to make 100 yourself and buy 100 when hunting.

Goals/Outcome/Consequences:
Pro's:Brings back a little of the rpg aspect and manasitting, chatting etc.

Cons: Cant really think of any, but feel free to comment

Added feedback:
Let runes cost less soulpoints or increase the 200 sp we have now so we can make enough runes to hunt for more than 20 mins.
