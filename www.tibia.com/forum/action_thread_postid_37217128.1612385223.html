and a scroll full of handwritten notes was lying atop of them:

Access to dungeons and bosses

  • Teleports to each dungeon can be accessed via a hellish place that works as some sort of hub and which is crawling with monsters.
  • Each dungeon is filled with monsters. In the middle part of each dungeon, you have to overcome a special obstacle to advance. This may either be a mini boss or some sort of riddle.
    Mini bosses can be fought by up to 5 players at the same time (teleport to mini boss via 5 tiles, 1 lever).
  • If you successfully complete this challenge (mini boss or riddle) you can proceed. Also, you can skip this special challenge from then on your way through the dungeon.
  • At the end of each dungeon you will reach the antechamber of the main boss room (where you have to fight the minion that guards this dungeon). A minion can be fought by up to 5 players at the same time (teleport to boss room via 5 tiles, 1 lever). Each boss fight has a time limit.
  • A player can fight each minion once every two days. This means: As soon as you have been teleported to the boss room of a dungeon via the antechamber, you have to wait 2 days before you can teleport to this boss room again. You can still fight against the minions in other dungeons, though.

  • Ferumbras' dungeon can be accessed after you have collected all seven pieces of the mysterious weapon from the seven dungeons. From then on, you can access Ferumbras' dungeon without having to beat all seven minions again.
  • Ferumbras' dungeon is also crawling with monsters. At the end of the dungeon you will reach the antechamber of his boss room. He can be fought by up to 15 players at the same time (teleport to boss room via 15 tiles, 1 lever). The boss fight has a time limit.
  • A player can fight the new Ferumbras once every 14 days.

    Rewards

  • If you complete the main questline, you will be rewarded with the base rift warrior outfit and an achievement.
  • All minions and the new Ferumbras will have special loot consisting of new items and already existing items. Check the Tibia facebook page to get a glimpse on some of the new items that may be dropped.
  • Moreover, the minions and the new Ferumbras will drop silver tokens. You will be able to exchange tokens for certain items with NPC Cledwyn. He will offer a new piece of armor for every vocation and the rift warrior addons in exchange for a certain number of silver tokens. Silver tokens are part of a new token system which may be expanded in future updates.
  • The rift mount will be obtainable via a rare monster drop in the dungeons.

    TL;DR: Teaser in a nutshell

  • seven themed dungeons inspired by the Ruthless Seven
  • dungeons not only harbour monster mobs but also confront players with other challenges such as riddles or mini bosses which they need to overcome to advance
  • at the end of each dungeon players have to fight a strong minion of the Ruthless Seven
  • repeatable bosses (each minion can be fought once every two days per player, new Ferumbras can be fought once every 14 days per player)
  • minions can be fought in teams up to 5 players, new Ferumbras in a team up to 15 players
  • new outfit and new mount
  • new items dropped via boss loot
  • bosses also drop silver tokens which are part of a new token system (which may be expanded in future updates)
  • NPC Cledwyn offers a new piece of armor for each vocation or rift warrior addons in exchange for silver tokens

    As already mentioned in the first teaser:

  • aimed at teams (high level content of various difficulty levels, reference for difficulty: PoI/Inquisition and higher)

    And please keep in mind: All things may still be subject to change since the update is still in development.
