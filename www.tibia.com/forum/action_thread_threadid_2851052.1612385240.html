Remove the 10 seconds to use Obsidian knife on own monsters!
Author
Thread #2851052 Pages: 1 2 3 4 ... Last Page < Previous Topic      Next Topic >
Bemagnon

 

As the topic says. We should be able to use obsidian knife and blessed wooden stake on our own monsters immediately not having to wait 10 seconds.
It's really annoying having to wait 10 seconds every time you kill a monster and people steal your monsters to skinn/dust them.
And now when the monsters decay faster u cant come back and skinn/dust later because then it's usually too late.
CipSoft, you gave us the right too loot our own monsters, which is great, now it's time to give us the right to skinn/dust our own mosters as well.

Note that only the one who has killed the monster (and his party members) should be able to skinn/dust immediately. Others still have to wait 10 seconds to avoid thieving.

I know there was a similar thread like this one before, but I couldn't find it anywhere on the forums so I made a new one.
Edit: Credit to Cooh who made this proposal before me.
