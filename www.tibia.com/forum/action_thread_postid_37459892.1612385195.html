Tldr

- Destructive forces are causing vortexes. These forces threaten to rip our world apart.
- Vortexes appear in different places through worldchanges.
- By pulling levers you can start different challenges. By mastering them, you will gain charges of energy that you will need to face the boss in the end.
- Even though this otherworld is full of monsters that don't require an army in order to get rid of them, it is good advise that if you try to really save the world, you go there with a team The endfight difficulty is comparable to PoI/Inquisition.
- There will also be a possibility for lower levels to experience parts of the otherworld.

And please keep in mind: All things may still be subject to change since the update is still in development.
