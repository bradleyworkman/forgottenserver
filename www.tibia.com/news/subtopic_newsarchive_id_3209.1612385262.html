Old Trails Lead to New Discoveries! (IX)
ay VIII...


Location: Kha'zeel
Risk: medium
Creature/Enemy activity: high
Notes:
I found an unknown cave in the mountains. A path led me deeper into Kha'zeel. Above me, I could hear the electrical crackling of energy elementals. I have to admit that it sounds more creepy down there than it usually does on the surface...

At the end of the path, I climbed down a hole and found an old and abandoned ruin. Even if most of the place is decayed and the corridors and walls have already collapsed, I can still see that this was once a magnificent and impressive building...
Nowadays, a stinking death awaits you. Poison fills the air and crawling creatures will be after your blood if you dare to enter...
