Monster Rampage
re you ready for some action, Tibians? Tomorrow afternoon, the test of the forthcoming winter update will be opened for all players who had premium status on November 16, 2012, 10:00 CET. While we are going to provide further information about this tomorrow, we already have some exciting news for you:

In order to celebrate the start of the public test, CMs, devs and testers will summon some fierce monsters to test the courage and strength of brave heroes and adventurers tomorrow.

Where to face your doom?

Testa: Thais Crossroads, December 04, from 16:00 until 17:00 CET approximately.

Testera: Thais Crossroads, December 04, from 17:00 until 18:00 CET approximately.

Do you have what it takes to defy the odds, and slay one of the evil foes? In any case, you better be ready to die!

See you on the battlefield!
