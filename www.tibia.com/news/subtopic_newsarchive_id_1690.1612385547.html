May 03 2011 -
Downtime Due to Maintenance and Small Patch
omorrow, May 04, all game worlds will be offline from 12:00 until 13:00 CEST approximately due to technical maintenance work at our American service provider. In addition, a client patch will be implemented during the downtime.

This client patch will address a couple of issues that came up with the recent spring patch. A console option to show or hide status messages which originate from other creatures and characters will be added, for example. Further information on important changes can be found in the Auditorium .

The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. If you should encounter any problems, you can alternatively download the new client 8.73 from our download section.

Thank you for your feeback!
