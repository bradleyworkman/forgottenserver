Tibia Payment System Available for More Countries
ven though Tibia is a free MMORPG, you can upgrade your account to a premium account by buying premium time. For 5 weeks, players from Sweden, Germany and France have been able to use the new Tibia payment system to buy premium time to enjoy all of Tibia's great premium features . As everything worked out well, we have decided to make the new payment system available for more countries. From tomorrow on, around noon, also players from the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Norway, Finland, Belgium, Austria, Denmark and Switzerland will be able to set up a subscription or to order single premium time periods of 30 or 90 days.

Of course, this is only the first step. We are planning to add further countries in the next months, so many more players can enjoy the great features of the Tibia payment system.
