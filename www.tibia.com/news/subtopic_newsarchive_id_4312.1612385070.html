Split Chat Feature for Tibia 11

reat news fellow Tibians, the Tibia 11 client received a new feature today!
Once you have updated your client to the latest version, you will be able to split your console window.

Just right-click on an open tab in your console and select "Show in Read-Only Tab" or drag and drop the tab of your choice to the new symbol in the upper-right corner of your console to create a read-only copy of the channel.

This feature can help you to keep track of your loot channel while you text with your friends in the guild chat, for example.
Keep in mind that if you close the original channel, the copy will be closed as well.

Furthermore, we have restructured the Premium Features section on our website and visually improved the account status in the client's character selection.

We hope you enjoy the new feature,
