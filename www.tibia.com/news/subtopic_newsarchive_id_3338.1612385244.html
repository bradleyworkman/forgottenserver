Blessings and Home Teleport Added to the Store
s announced last week, blessings and home temple teleport have been added to the Store as new products and are now available for Tibia Coins.

If you would like to save a bit of time while playing, these products may come in handy. They are consumables which means they have a one-time effect and then need to be purchased again.
If you want to get further information on a product before buying, select it in the Store and then click on "Details" to learn more about this specific product.

In order to further optimise Dawnport and the tutorial, we have also implemented a few small adjustments such as additional hints and tool tips with today's server save.

Please note that a new client version is available. The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. You can also download the new stand-alone client version 10.82 from our download section .

Have fun!
