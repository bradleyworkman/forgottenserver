The Secret Cults of Tibia

t first he thought one of his mates had spiked his last beer at Frodo's for a laugh or that some evil witch had cast a spell on him but one thing he was certain about, this was not happening in his head. The stone, the tree, those animals, they were all talking to him in human language but Tibicus could not believe what they had to say.

It was the druid, Gerimor was his name, who had divulged the alarming secret to him concerning the existence of covert cults all over Tibia. Cultists with evil intentions, some of them not even human, worshipping false gods and promises! They differ in ideology and goals and are unaware of each other’s existence. The time is ripe to infiltrate their inner circle and stop their sinister plans! Because, whatever they are plotting, it would have fatal consequences for all denizens in Tibia if they were to succeed!

Gather together all you outlaws, scoundrels and bandits! You were treated like outcasts long enough! You are thirsting for gold, wealth and power! And you shall have it all!
You are all alone in this world! Each and every one is conspiring against you! Kill them! Kill them all! Let go of your previous worthless earthly life and seek salvation!

Life is precious, too precious to fade away like sand that slips through your fingers! Green... green is hope! Prevent the sand from slipping! Tighten your grasp and force it to stay in place!

Tibicus had to investigate these rumours! But what if all of it turns out to be true and there are conspirators among the Tibian population? Then whom can he trust any longer? Who are the leaders behind the cults and how did they manage to hide themselves in the dark for so long?
