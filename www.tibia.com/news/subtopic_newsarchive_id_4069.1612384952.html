Retro Outfits

emember the old character outfits? It has been more than ten years already, since they were re-designed!
Well, you cannot celebrate a retro month if you have no suitable clothes to wear! Therefore, it is time for their long awaited revival. A first batch of four retro outfits has been added to the Store today!

Prepare your character to walk the streets of the upcoming two new Retro Open PvP game worlds as a Retro Citizen! 
Sharpen your sword and polish your shield before you throw yourself fearlessly into battle to protect your party members as a Retro Knight.
If you are more interested in distance fighting with arrows and spears, the Retro Hunter should be your matching outfit.
Last but not least you can dress up as a  Retro Mage and you will always cut a fine figure on the battleground while eliminating your enemies with your magical powers!

Once bought, your character can wear these outfits anytime. The outfits are purely cosmetic and do not bestow any benefits.

Stay tuned for more to come!
