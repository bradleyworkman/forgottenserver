New Outfits Available
wo new fancy outfits have been added to the Outfits section in the payment system!

In total, 8 new combinations are up for grabs with prices ranging from 13.95 EUR to 29.95 EUR.
If your character is into dark magic, the chaos acolyte outfit is a perfect way to express your inner nature. Those who do not mind the smell of decay and the thick claws of a vulture may prefer the grim death herald outfit, though.

In order to get a better idea of how these outfits will look ingame, head over to the Outfits section in the payment system and simply place your mouse pointer over an outfit to start a walking animation that will show you both the female and male version from all directions.

Once bought and assigned to one of your characters, you can wear the outfit ingame anytime, no matter if you are a free account or a premium account. Please note that these outfits are purely cosmetic and do not bestow any benefits.
