Dec 04 2014 -
Content Fixes and Changes
ith today's server save, we implemented a few content fixes and changes, for example:
  • We addressed an issue with the mission 6 of The Ape City Quest. Players can now obtain the needed item once every 24 hours by using "some witches' cap mushrooms".
  • A small issue with NPC Perod and his trade dialogue has been fixed as well.
  • Furthermore, the animations of some mounts have been adjusted.
  • Also, some other graphic as well as map bugs, and typos have been fixed.
Please note that a new client version is available. The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. You can also download the new stand-alone client version 10.64 from our download section .
