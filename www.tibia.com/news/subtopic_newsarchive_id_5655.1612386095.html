Jul 19 2020 -
News Ticker

Around 16:00 CEST, all game worlds have to be taken offline for about 20 minutes to implement an urgent quick fix to address the exploitive farming of the boss Fugue. The quick fix will adjust the loot appropriately. Within the course of the next week, we will take a closer look at the excessive farming that took place to be able to assess the situation properly and evaluate our options.
