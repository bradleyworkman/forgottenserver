Outfits Available via Payment System
o you want to treat your character to a new look? If so, check out the newly added Outfits section in the payment system!

Three distinguished outfits are exclusively available there and each of them can be spiced up further with fancy addons. In total, 12 combinations are up for grabs with prices ranging from 12.95 EUR to 28.95 EUR.

The champion outfit perfectly suits battle-hardened warriors who rely on their trusty sword and shield. Disciples of magic may prefer to wear the elaborate conjurer outfit next time they call on the elements. Those of you who are looking for a faithful companion who can also bare his teeth at your enemies may take a liking to the beastmaster outfit.

In order to get a better idea of how these outfits will look ingame, head over to the Outfits section in the payment system and simply place your mouse pointer over an outfit to start a walking animation that will show you both the female and male version from all directions.

Once bought and assigned to one of your characters, you can wear the outfit ingame anytime, no matter if you are a free account or a premium account. Please note that these outfits are purely cosmetic and do not bestow any benefits.
