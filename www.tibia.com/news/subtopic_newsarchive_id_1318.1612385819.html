Client Patch 8.56
oday, the announced client patch has been released and further reporting options have been implemented.

You are now able to report illegal public statements ingame as well as players you suspect of using unofficial software to play. For further instructions on how to do this, please have a look at the FAQ entries for statement reports and bot reports .

The patch will be downloaded automatically when you log into the game. You can alternatively download the new client 8.56 from our download section in case you encounter any problems.
