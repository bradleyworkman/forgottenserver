The Update Is Online!
s promised the update is online now - featuring many new areas, like Ab'Dendriel the town of the elves, the Ice Islands in the cold north of Tibia and the Fields of Glory. Additionally many things have changed in the known parts of the continent. So prepare yourself to find new mysteries and challenges everywhere!

Tibia has now four different home towns you can move to: Thais, Ab'Dendriel, Kazordoon and Carlin. Choose the city you like best to make it your home.

We also want to thank all editors and testers for their passion and time. Without you the update would have never been as good as it is!

Have fun in Tibia!
