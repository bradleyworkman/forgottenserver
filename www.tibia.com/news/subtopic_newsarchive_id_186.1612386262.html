Minor Game Update
ome bugs in the server code and minor weaknesses of the game have been fixed. The only important change is that you will receive only 50% of the experience points of your summoned or convinced monsters from now on. Also, you will miss one strike if you change your tactics during a fight. On the other hand, a mana shield will now also protect you against life drain of vampires and monsters alike.

Have fun in Tibia!
