Oct 13 2020 -
News Ticker

Today's patch fixes a bug which caused some NPC trade menus to open with an empty sell section. Another change that has been made is that when a player has posted in the Auditorium, they now can only post with that same character in the next 24 hours. If they want to post with another character, they have to wait 24 hours from their last post on before they can do so.
