Character Information & Cyclopedia Map Walk

ith the upcoming winter update we will introduce another expansion to the Cyclopedia: The Character Information provides a detailed overview of your character's current stats.

This includes well-known information from the Skills window as well as new overviews of the current attack, defence, and armor values and positive respectively negative influences from equipped items which provide additional elemental damage/resistances or other effects.

In addition, hovering with your mouse cursor over your skill level will provide you with a more detailed tooltip. The loyalty bonus is going to be displayed separately in those tips making it possible to find out your plain skill level, as it is listed in the highscores.

     

Furthermore, you can access a list of all character deaths of the last 30 days as well as a list of all kills of the last 70 days directly in the client.

In the Achievements section you can see all regular and already completed secret achievements of your character. The achievements can be sorted by unlock date, alphabetically or by grade.

Another cool new feature brings walking via map click to the Cyclopedia Map. SHIFT+Left Click in your Cyclopedia Map and your character will start walking to the desired position as long as the destination is reachable, within range and on the same layer.
