Jan 30 2018 -

Bugfixes and Content Changes

e released some bugfixes and content changes today.

  • The initial protection of items with activated imbuement slots should now also be applied correctly to bleeding damage.
  • The following monsters were added to the prey system: minotaur cult follower, minotaur cult prophet and minotaur cult zealot.
  • The following monsters can now push items and other creatures if they block their way: ravenous lava lurker, cave devourer, deepworm, tunnel tyrant.
  • The spell "Conjure Arrow" can now be learned from the respective NPCs again.
  • The makeshift warrior outfit received some minor graphical fixes.
