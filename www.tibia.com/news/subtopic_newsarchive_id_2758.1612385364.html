May 20 2014 -
Content Fixes
ith today's server save, we implemented a few content fixes. For example:

  • Secret Service quest: The two signet rings in the different secret service quests are now distinguishable by name. The TBI version is called "suspicious signet ring" and the AVIN version "family signet ring". The AVIN version is now also obtainable via Black Bert.
  • Gravedigger of Drefia quest: Players are now bounced if they do not have 2 half-eaten brains with them when using the brain machine entrance teleport.

    • Together with the content fixes, a new client version is available. The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. You can also download the new stand-alone client version 10.4 from our download section .

    Enjoy!
