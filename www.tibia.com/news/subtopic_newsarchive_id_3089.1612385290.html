Celebrate Carnival in a Ceremonial Garb
n February, carnival is celebrated in many countries, and also in Tibia.

If you want to make a great entrance at a Tibian costume party, the ceremonial garb is certainly a good choice. With a drum over your shoulder and adorned with feathers you are perfectly dressed to lead a carnival parade through the streets of Thais.

You can find the ceremonial garb in the Outfits section in the payment system. Once bought and assigned to one of your characters, you can wear the outfit ingame anytime, no matter if you are a free account or a premium account. This outfit is purely cosmetic and does not bestow any benefits.
