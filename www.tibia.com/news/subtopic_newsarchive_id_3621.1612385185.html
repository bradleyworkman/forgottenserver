Jun 14 2016 -
Content Fixes and Changes
ith today's server save, we implemented some content fixes and changes. For example:
  • You can no longer enter the entrance area of the Ankrahmun PvE arena with a protection zone block.
  • The chance to get a pit demon doll from a surprise cube has been increased.
  • You can now use the exit teleporters in the heart of destruction areas without having lost your protection zone block.
  • Unstable sparks, as well as some other event monsters, underwent some adjustments. Also, if you exceed the time limit there, you are now kicked out to the correct place.
  • The bosses Rupture, Eradicator and Outburst can now be repeated after 20 hours.
  • You can no longer walk over Store items like pets or light sources.
  • We fixed a few minor issues such as typos.
See you in Tibia!
