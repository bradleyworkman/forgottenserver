Dec 04 2018 -

Bugfixes and Content Changes

ith today's server save, we have released a new client version. Among others, we changed the following:

  • An issue which logged in the wrong character in the characters selection screen when hidden characters are not displayed has been resolved.
  • An issue causing the character selection screen to malfunction under certain circumstances has been fixed.
  • Takeable crystal lamps were removed from the first floor of the carnivora dungeon.
  • Health points, attack and defence values of monsters inhabiting warzone 4, 5 and 6 have been reduced.
  • Heavy crystal fragments can now be obtained at a faster pace and as loot from the warzone bosses.
  • Raised the drop chances of the item suspicious device .

Have fun,
Your Community Managers
