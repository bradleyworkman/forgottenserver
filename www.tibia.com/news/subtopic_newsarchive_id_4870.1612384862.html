Dec 18 2018 -

Bugfixes and Content Changes

ith today's server save, we have released a new client version. Among others, we changed the following:

  • The enchanted pendulet no longer vanishes but turns into a rechargeable pendulet when its timer runs out. 
  • The giant amethyst was renamed to giant sapphire .
  • Faceless Bane should now be easier to kill.
  • Several rubbish items, which were added to the Market last week, are now listed in the correct category.
  • Multiple protection zone fields were removed from the "Path of Defiances" dungeon.
  • An issue causing Alptramun to become invincible under certain circumstances has been resolved.
  • Hitting invisible characters with a snowball no longer turns them into snowmen. 

Have fun,
Your Community Managers
