Jan 09 2018 -

Bugfixes and Content Changes

ith today's server save, we updated our Tibia client and added some changes: 

  • Fixed several issues that caused some events from our 20 years of Tibia celebration to reappear.
  • An issue causing occasional FPS drops while Quick Looting has been resolved.
  • NPC Grizzly Adams is now listed as Sell/Buy NPC in the Cyclopedia.
  • The gold display is now updated every time you close the Bestiary and open it again.
  • We added missing achievements for mount "Mole" and the Makeshift Warrior outfit.
  • Freshly killed corpses of several new monsters are no longer moveable by everyone.
  • The following monsters have been removed from the Bestiary: Elf Overseer, Insectoid Scout, Dryad, Vicious Manbat, Nightfiend.
  • We increased the time to rescue captured dwarfs before they disappear.
  • Explosive Growth: Adjusted explosion radius in room three and four.

Have fun,
Your Community Managers
