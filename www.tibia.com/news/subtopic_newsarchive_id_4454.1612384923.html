New Decoration

isten up all you proud house owners! New decoration has been added to the Store! Alchemy is the slogan for various pieces of furniture that you can use to decorate your home. Most importantly for an alchemist is of course the Alchemistic Table and the matching Alchemistic Scales .

Furthermore, a cupboard and a Pile of Alchemistic Books  as well as a bookstand, a chair and a cabinet complete the new Alchemist interior.
The Light of Change as well as the Torch of Change can set the right mood with their different lightening colours.

There are also new paintings , you can decorate your walls with. Some of you will probably be familiar with the artworks.
Busts of famous personalities are now also available. Especially those who can call his hat their own should take a special look at the Ferumbras bust.  

When bought, these items are directly delivered to you as decoration kits, which can be unwrapped inside your house. The unwrapped decoration items are non-takeable and also indestructible.
