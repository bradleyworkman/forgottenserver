Aug 30 2016 -
New Store Products and Categories
he new products we introduced last week hit the store today and are now available for Tibia Coins. For further information please read the corresponding news and check the product details in the Store.

New Decoration
You can now have your own dog to decorate your home. He has his own dog house, a space where he can feel safe and cosy while his owner is out slaughtering monsters.
There is also a fish tank available for those who are in a more tranquil mood and enjoy watching fishes gliding through the water.

Does your house need some renovation? Perfect time to roll up your sleeves! Brand new floors arrived and you can now decorate your home in country style with rustic furniture, while new tapestries add a nice touch to your walls.

New Store Categories
If new products as those listed above are added, they are now also shown in the category New Products . The category Special Offers will be displayed if there are any products on sale which are available for your character. If no new products or special offers are available, the category Featured Products will be displayed, showing a certain selection of available products.



In addition, we implemented a bunch of bug fixes . Please also note that a new client version is available. The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. You can also download the new stand-alone client version 10.97 from our download section .

Have fun!
