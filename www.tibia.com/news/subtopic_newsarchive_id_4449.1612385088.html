Jan 16 2018 -

Bugfixes and Content Changes

ime for another round of bugfixes and content changes:

  • Adding imbuements to your equipment no longer compromises the initial resistances.
  • It is no longer possible to leave a boss fight in the new warzones early in order to start it a second time.
  • We fixed an issue concerning the participation system of the "Baron From Below".
  • The Achievement "Hunting Permit" is now also awarded if the first completed Bestiary entry refers to a creature with the difficulty level trivial.
  • We renamed "lava lurkers" in the gnomish area to "ravenous lava lurkers" to avoid mix-ups.
  • We added sparkling effects to "blessed acorn" and "sacred antler talisman".
  • If players are standing in a stack, only the player on top of the stack can shoot with diamond arrows from now on.
  • Summons can no longer participate in the fight against Zamulosh.
  • "Panpipes" can now be sold to NPC Valindara and NPC Talila.
  • Monster "skinning" should now work as intended again.
  • Threatened Dreams: Starlight Vial, Moon Mirror and Sun Catcher bought from NPC Black Bert can now be charged to continue the quest.
  • Some minor map and NPC issues were fixed.
