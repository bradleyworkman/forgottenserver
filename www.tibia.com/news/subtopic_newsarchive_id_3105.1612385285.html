Mar 03 2015 -
Content Fixes and Changes
ith today's server save, we implemented a few content fixes and changes, for example:
  • The requirements to open the Dark Grounds in Roshamuul have been adjusted to make it easier to gain access to the area.
  • An issue with the spawn of Gaz'haragoth has been fixed. He spawns now as intended.
  • Unreachable wall decorations behind depots in guild halls have been sent to the inbox of the guildleader.
  • In addition, some graphical issues and typos have been fixed and adjusted as well.
  • Last but not least, it is now possible to use hotkeys in the stand-alone client to equip your character.
Please note that a new client version is available. The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. You can also download the new stand-alone client version 10.76 from our download section .

See you in Tibia!
