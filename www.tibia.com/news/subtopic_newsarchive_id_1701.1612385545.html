Content Fix
ith tomorrow's server save, a fix for the mad sheep issue will be implemented. A character will only be able to summon a mad sheep about twice a day. Also, if your character is in a protection zone you will be able to remove a mad sheep by using one of the following items on it: shovel, rope, light shovel, elvenhair rope, whacking driller of fate, squeezing gear of girlpower or sneaky stabber of eliteness.

If you want to talk about the change with other players, you can leave your comments in the thread Player Discussions: Content Fixes .
