Price Adjustments for Consumable Items

ellow Tibians,

while the prices for potions and runes have not changed since their introduction, over the years newer and more profitable hunting grounds were introduced to Tibia. Therefore, there is a growing disproportion between the high purchasing power of players and the low prices of some consumable items.

In order to meet this increasing purchasing power on our game worlds, we have made some adjustments to NPC and related Store items of various consumable items with today's server save.

Affected by the adjustments are all potions, exercise weapons, some runes and all casks and kegs.

We have prepared a full list of all affected items and their new prices here .

Those changes are effective immediately.

See you in Tibia,
Your Community Managers
