Nov 24 2020 -
News Ticker

Today's patch fixes and changes a couple of things: Knights can now buy strong mana kegs in the Store and use them, while paladins can buy great mana kegs in the Store and use them. Quivers can no longer be sold to NPCs. Magic Shield is now given in absolute numbers in the status bar and no longer as a percentage. The descriptions of Protector and Blood Rage in the spell library have been changed to reflect the latest changes.
