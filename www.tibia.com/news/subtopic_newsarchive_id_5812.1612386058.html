News Ticker

Among other things, today's patch fixed and changed the following: Text copied from the console will now begin with the oldest message again and end with the newest one. Also, the ancient lion knight moved out of his tower to the beach where he will mingle with the usurpers from now on. Moreover, a tooltip in the "Regular World Transfer" entry of Char Bazaar listings now informs you about the restrictions of world transfers.
