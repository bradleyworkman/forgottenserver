Bug Fixes Tibia 11
ately, we were able to address a lot of issues due to your feedback. Today, we released another round of fixes for Tibia 11.
  • We improved the visibility of the health bar. Numeric effects should no longer cover the bar.
  • Containers (e.g. backpacks) now close without delay.
  • To improve its visibility, the mouse cursor was brightened up.
  • Left Smart-Click now opens a container in the same window. Additionally, ALT+Left Smart-Click opens a container in a new window now.
  • Monsters and NPCs displayed in the battle window are now listed in the correct order.
  • Further improvements concerning turning with CTRL while moving.
Thank you all for reporting. It helps a lot. Keep it coming!
