May 10 2002 -
Survey for the New Client
he current silence from our side is the first sign for big changes in Tibia. We work intensively on two updates: the new client and new map areas.

For the further development of the new client, we would like to ask you some questions about your hardware and operating system in a survey . This information will help to optimise the client for your needs.

As indicated in the last news we will present a new big area for premium players with a new town, more monsters, and fantastic dungeons. Moreover some areas like Kazordoon have been reworked and enlarged. All players can encounter new secrets and challenges there.

Finally we want to welcome two new gamemasters: Flattery and Ygrane . Our team of gamemasters will watch and help the Tibian community. A big thank you to all of them!

Have fun in Tibia!
Your CipSoft Team
