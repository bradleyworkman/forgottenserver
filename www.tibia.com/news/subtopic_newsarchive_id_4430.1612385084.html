Dec 19 2017 -

Bugfixes and Content Changes

ime for another round of bugfixes and content changes:

  • We removed the following creatures from the Bestiary: Charger, Cursed Gladiator, Dawn Bat, Death Reaper, Depolarized Crackler, Dwarf Henchman, Ghost of a Planegazer, Harbinger of Darkness, Lesser Swarmer, Lizard Gate Guardian, Murderous Ghost, Mushroom Sniffer, Overcharge, Squidgy Slime, Swarmer Hatchling, Unstable Spark, Vortex Spawn, Young Troll.
  • Makeshift Warrior outfit addons and suspicious device are now listed in the correct Market category.
  • You can now wear a suspicious device as an amulet.
  • Powerful Featherweight imbuement now requires five "Goosebump Leather" instead of "Fig Leaf".
  • We fixed the character riding animation on the new mount.
  • Further adjustments concerning the loot drop chances of diremaw and deepworm have been made.
  • We reworked the Character World Transfer dialog of NPC Sharon.
  • Explosive Barrel quest: Increased maximum amount of coexisting barrels.
  • Last but not least, we fixed an issue causing the client to crash.

Enjoy,
Your Community Managers
