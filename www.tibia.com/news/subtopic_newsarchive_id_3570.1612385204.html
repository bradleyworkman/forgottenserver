May 03 2016 -
Store Potions on Sale
ver been right in the middle of a hunting session when you suddenly noticed that you are running low on potions?

If you know the feeling, our current potion sale in the Store may be of interest to you: All potions are currently available at 10-20% off their regular price!

This sale lasts for a limited time only. So grab those potions while they are marked with a sale banner in the Store to benefit from this special offer!

Moreover, we implemented a few fixes and adjustments today, for example:
  • Chartan has stocked up his shelves: He also offers the new potions now. Gnomegica, on the other hand, no longer sells them.
  • We finally persuaded Ferumbras to drop his staff if his mortal shell gets defeated.
  • We addressed an issue that had a few characters trapped in Santiago's quest on Rookgaard. These characters have been moved to Dawnport and can continue their Tibian journey from there.
  • Crystal tables can now be rewrapped properly.
  • If you do not fulfil any of the required conditions to buy a certain product in the Store, you can now see which conditions you do not meet: Either place the mouse pointer on the corresponding product and wait for the tool tip to open or select a product and click on "Details" to check it.
  • We fixed an issue that made it possible for paladins to buy knight exclusive health potions in the Store.
Please note that a new client version is available. The patch will download automatically when you log into the game. You can also download the new stand-alone client version 10.93 from our download section .

See you in Tibia!
