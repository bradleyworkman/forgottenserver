Longer Account Numbers and Revised Documentation
ince the Tibian community is expanding quickly we need further account numbers. From now on account numbers may contain 6 or 7 digits. For this reason an update of the Tibia client is necessary. When you start your client a patch will be downloaded automatically. If you encounter any problems, you can alternatively download the new client from our website.

In addition to this client update we have also revised parts of our documentation. We have worked once more on the manual , the quickstart and the FAQ . They should now provide you with enhanced information. Moreover, you can take a look at the first illustrations by our graphic artist in the manual section on vocations .

Have fun in Tibia!
