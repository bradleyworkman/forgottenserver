Let's Take a Walk!
re you ready for a whole new walking experience? With the forthcoming summer update, we are going to implement smooth walking animations for all player outfits. The following two outfit examples will give you a better idea on how this will look ingame.

Female barbarian before and after the summer update:
      


Male assassin before and after the summer update:
      


The summer update will only mark the first step of this graphical improvement. Other creatures such as monsters and smoother animations while riding on a mount will follow later on in future updates.

We also have some useful information for all players who are looking forward to the upcoming test of the summer update. While the exact dates of the private test and the public test are yet to be announced, we can already tell you the date of the character backup that will be used for the test: June 27, 10:00 CEST.
