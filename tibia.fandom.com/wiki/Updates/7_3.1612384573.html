Major Updates

Summary

Ankrahmun was introduced as well as an automatic spam control system.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • A new land and city has emerged in the continent of Darama . Now traveling south of the city Darashia to the Plague Spike a new mountain pass gives access to the desert on the other side and the city of Ankrahmun .
  • Minor graphic changes were introduced.
  • An automatic spam control system.
    • Only one message every two minutes can be send in the trade channel.
    • A limit of messages that can be said within a certain period of times was implemented.
    • If you're muted from speaking in muted channels, you're also muted from casting spells.
  • You can now buy and sell multiple items at the same time from NPCs .
