Update Teasers

  1. New Tutor System

Changes

  • You can now receive Thank you!'s from other players for helpful posts and statements.
  • By receiving enough Thank you!'s from other players in a specific timeframe and fulfil some minor requirements, you will automatically become a tutor.

Flash Client Changes

Furthermore, the Flash Client has been updated to Flash Player plugin version 11.0. In addition, a couple of minor bugs and issues were fixed in this client as well. For example:

  • The size of the container widget does not change unexpectedly anymore when dragging the widget or opening another container.
  • The "Click to activate" message has been moved to the top half of the window so that it does not hide the content of client messages anymore.
