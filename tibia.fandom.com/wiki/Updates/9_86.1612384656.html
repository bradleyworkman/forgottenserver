Summary

The update goal was to implement what has been discussed in the Tibia Council — new hunting grounds, as well as changes to existing ones and loot adjustments for a few creatures.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Many new areas were added.
  • Existing areas were changed.
  • The loot of some creatures were improved.
  • The spell behavior and armor of Carniphilas has been changed
  • Buddel has been moved from east of the barbarian camp to north of the barbarian camp

New areas

  • Darama
    • Darama Terramite Cave — Terramite dungeon with entrances on Kha'labal ( here ) and Tiquanda ( here ).
    • Tiquanda Laboratory — Mutated Human and Bio-Elementals area, here .
  • Hrodmir and the northern ice islands
    • Nibelor Ice Cave — Ice Golem and Crystal Spider dungeon in Nibelor , 2 entrances: here and here .
    • Hrodmir Quara Scout Caves — two Quara Scout dungeons: here and here .
    • Frost Dragon Tunnel — short tunnel with Frost Dragons , Frost Dragon Hatchlings and Crystal Spiders ( here ), connects the entrance floor of the Formorgar Troll Caves with the Formorgar Mines .
    • Ice Witch Temple — Ice Golem , Crystal Spider , Ice Witch and Frost Dragon cave, here .
    • Mammoth Shearing Factory — Mammoth , Frost Giant and Witch cave, here .
  • Yalahar
    • War Golem area in the Factory Quarter . Extends the Worker Golem spawn ( here , extension starts here ). Note : use the machine either to the left or to the right to sacrifice 4 Gear Wheels and then step on the platform to go to the respective area below. When below, you can hunt for as long as you want. To return, step in the electric field near the start of the area you are in.
  • Zao
    • Fire Dragon Dojo — Lizard Chosen dungeon under the Temple of Equilibrium in Muggy Plains ( here ).
    • A new Gnarlhound dungeon separate from the other Gnarlhound Caves , in the Zao Steppe ( here ).

Changes to existing areas

  • Oasis Tomb
    • The second floor now has 16 Gargoyles .
  • Edron Wyvern Hill
  • Edron Troll Cave
    • Entrance received a small cave extension with a Rope quest chest to prevent players from getting stuck ( here ).
  • Laguna Islands
  • Green Claw Swamp
    • Small cave with 3 Tarantulas added here .
  • Tiquanda
    • The existing Carniphila Cave has been expanded.
    • Tarantulas and Poison Spiders have been added to the lower floors of the Tiquanda Tarantula Caves . Toads and Centipedes have been moved to caverns to the sides.
  • Barbarian Settlements
    • Buddel moved from here to here .
    • The amount of Mammoths has significantly increased.
    • Several Penguins replaced with Badgers .
  • Formorgar Glacier
    • Existing spawn of 6 Crystal Spiders and Ice Golems has been extended and now contains 24 Crystal Spiders and 18 Ice Golems , here .
    • The teleporter to Bloodweb has been relocated, it is now guarded by four Crystal Spiders .
  • Formorgar Mines
    • Royal Mailbox on the first floor ( here ).
    • Several Cultists replaced with Stone Golems , Ice Golems and Destroyers .
    • More Gargoyles and Crystal Spiders have been added to the mines.
    • 11-12 Hellspawns were added to a specific part of the mine, here . This part also contains Nightmare Scions .
  • Hrodmir Stone Golem Caves
    • Second floor added to the north cave, also with Gargoyles , here .
  • Factory Quarter
    • The existing War Golem spawn received 3-4(?) golems.
  • Razachai
  • Zao Steppe
    • Underground Gnarlhound Caves have been expanded on several areas, Centipedes have been replaced with Gnarlhounds on the lower floors.
    • A Lancer Beetle cave that was previously part of the Gnarlhound caves has been split off and expanded, it now contains roughly 15 Lancer Beetles , here .

Improved

  • Hand of Cursed Fate
    • Drops Sudden Death Rune 's now.
  • Hellhound
    • Assassin Star drop improved. Drops rares and potions more frequently.
  • Ice Golem
    • Drops Ice Cubes and potions more often. Drops Strong Mana Potion .
  • Juggernaut
  • Lost Soul
    • Drops Red Gem now.
  • Mammoth
    • Higher drop rates for most items.
  • Phantasm
    • Higher drop rate of gems now. Also drops more gems.
  • Quara Constrictor Scout
    • Gold drop improved, now drops Shrimp
  • Quara Hydromancer Scout
    • Gold drop improved, now drops Shrimp , Obsidian Lance
  • Quara Pincher Scout
  • Quara Mantassin Scout
  • Quara Predator Scout
    • Drops Shrimp , Two-Handed Sword , Northwind Rod
  • Stone Golem
    • Gold drop improved.
  • Terramite
    • Drops creature products more often.
  • Thornback Tortoise
  • Deathbine
  • The Bloodtusk
    • Drops Red Piece of Cloth .
