Updates/10.81

Minor patch 10.81 was released on August 11, 2015 , to implement some content fixes and changes.

Changes

  • Werebears can no longer harm other creatures.
  • Wereboars ' melee-lowering spell now properly targets Knights instead of Paladins .
  • The 22 hour limit for Killing in the Name of... Quest bosses on Retro Open PvP Worlds has been removed as the problem of being able to kill bosses without losing boss points was solved.
  • An issue which made it possible for players to kill Deathstrike twice in a row has been fixed.
  • Recruiter Outfits , Sea Dog Outfits and a mount have been implemented:
    • Tibia Horse (looks the same as the Rented Horse ).
  • Some graphical issues and map bugs were also fixed.
