Summary

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Fixed kicks and graphical cloning issues introduced by the previous update.

Compensation

Several days after the bug was fixed, CipSoft offered compensation to 699 accounts, giving them 7 days of premium time.

They identified the accounts on two requirements:

  • a crash report was sent to CipSoft
  • the character that suffered from the reported crash died during the time the client version 8.51 was online.
