Summary

A small patch that introduced some adjustments in respawns, and NPC.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • The number of Earth Elementals in the Tiquanda jungle has been decreased.
  • Jessica and Odemara have both added goblets to their sales list.
  • Some spelling mistakes in NPC dialogues were fixed.
  • Spellwands cannot be used on invisible characters anymore.
  • A few minor technical corrections on the client have been made along with several small bug fixes concerning map or graphic bugs.
  • The Wisps around Ab'Dendriel were removed.
  • The walls of the Kazordoon Iron Guildhall can now be decorated with wall decorations .
  • Goblin Assassins and Goblin Scavengers cannot be summoned nor convinced anymore.
  • Lily  now buys Empty Potion Flasks .
