Summary

This patch fixed various bugs and weaknesses in the game.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Children of the Revolution Quest : An issue with NPC Chartan was addressed. It is now possible again to finish his tasks.
  • The Repenters Quest : NPC Ashari now reacts correctly to all keywords.
  • An issue with the use of Dream Nebuliser in the Ancient Dreams was addressed. The Nebuliser now works as intended.
  • Changing the light settings in the Stand-Alone Client no longer affects creature names and health bar .
  • The use of hotkeys while walking in the Flash Client does not affect the speed of player characters anymore.
  • An issue with the Migration Tool in Google Chrome has been addressed. It should now be possible to use this browser without any problems.
  • Some typos and map bugs were fixed.
  • Two additional rewards to A Piece of Cake .
