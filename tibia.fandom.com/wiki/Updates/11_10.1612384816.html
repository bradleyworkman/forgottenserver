Updates/11.10

Version 11.10 was released on February 7, 2017 , together with a Tibia 11 patch .

Changes

Somewhere between this update and Updates/11.03 , some new sprites were added to the client. Furthermore market properties were added to the items Birthday Backpack , Birthday Cake , Ferumbras' Candy Hat , Chocolatey Dragon Scale Legs , Tatty Dragon Scale Legs , Rusty Winged Helmet , Filthy Bunnyslippers , Old Rag , Starfish (Item) , Shell (Item) , Blurred Transcript , Dragon Blood , Dragon Tongue , Scale of Gelidrazah , Tooth of Tazhadur , Horn of Kalyassa , Bones of Zorvorax , Bottle of Tibian Wine , Costume Bag , Framed Birthday Card , Gilded Birthday Cup , Porcelain Mask and Colourful Feathers .

Also, the animation delay of the image of Parrot (Item) was slightly changed.

The objects with ids 24501, 24502, 24503, 24710, 24711, 24712, 24726, 24727, 24728 received the "immobile" property.

Besides the items listed below also the Tapestry of Honour 11, 12, 13, 14 and 15 images were added (item ids 25205, 25206, 25207, 25208 and 25209).
