Updates/10.92

Minor patch 10.92 was released on April 12, 2016 to implement some content fixes and changes (see this news item and this news item ).

Changes

  • Ultimate Mana Potion , Ultimate Spirit Potion , and Supreme Health Potion were introduced.
  • The Store was expanded and now sells packs of potions , runes , and some unique furniture too.
  • The Store Inbox was implemented.
  • All non-takeable pieces of furniture can now be rewrapped. Store items will appear as Decoration Kits inside your Store Inbox whereas all other furniture will appear as Furniture Kits inside your inventory .
  • Furniture kits can no longer be traded on The Market .
  • The Convention section was removed from the Tibia Website .
  • Yasir also buys Creature Products dropped by Ogres now.
  • An error with the effect when skinning a Lizard High Guard was fixed.
  • You can now lose Simon the Beggar's Favorite Staff and still be able to complete the Beggar Outfits Quest .
  • Ferumbras' Ascension Quest : correctly pulling the levers in Mazoran 's room is always possible now.
  • The I Did My Part achievement can now be obtained through the Rise of Devovorga World Quest again.
  • Second mission of Jack to the Future Quest as well as the requirement to obtain Interior Decorator achievement were changed.
  • NPC Edvard now sells used furniture.
