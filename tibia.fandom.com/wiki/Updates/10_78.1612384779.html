Updates/10.78

Minor patch 10.78 was released on May 15, 2015 , to implement some smaller changes.

Changes

  • NPC Rahkem sells healing spells without any issues once again.
  • It is now impossible to travel from Port Hope to Oramond with a logout block .
  • Fansite Items have been removed from the Market .
  • One outfit and 3 new mounts have been implemented (see below).
  • Other graphical fixes, map bugs, and typos have been implemented.
