Summary

Express Character World Transfer and a dozen of Mounts were introduced to the Store .

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Improved animations, see Updates/10.51/Sprite Changes
  • Some fixes.
  • Express Character World Transfer , which costs twice as much as ordinary World Transfer
  • 12 new Mounts are now available for sale on tibia.com, priced between 15 and 30 euros. Some mounts are variations on already existing mounts or creatures. Desert King , Jade Lion and Winter King are all mountable Lions . Tombstinger , Death Crawler and Jade Pincer are variations on the King Scorpion . Platesaurian , Azudocus and Carpacosaurus are variations on the Dragonling . Nethersteed , Tempest and Flamesteed are however, not based on any Tibian creature or mount implemented so far.
