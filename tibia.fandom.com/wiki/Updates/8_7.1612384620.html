Summary

This update introduced Mounts , a new Cooldown system as well as other minor changes.

See also: Updates Help

  2. The Animal Whisperer

Changes

  • Revamped areas
    • Colossus Fortress in Kazordoon .
    • Bonelord homes in Hellgate and Drefia .
    • Outlaw Camp .
  • Mounts was introduced as a kind of outfit with 10 levels speed bonus. Most mounts are obtainable only by premium players after using certain items (dropped by creatures) on monsters. NPC Lothar will give more information about taming.
  • New spells for all vocations.
  • New status condition: Bleeding
  • New Cooldown system for spells. Stronger spells have a longer delay.
  • Lured creatures walk back to their respawn places as soon as they lose their target.
  • Players are not able to rope wild creatures any more.
  • Players are able to pass through other players in protection zones on all game world types.
  • Changes in channels - RL-Chat and Game-Chat has been merged and named World-Chat, Trade has been named Advertising and Default-Chat has become Local-Chat.
  • Intense Healing runes and Ultimate Healing runes colors were changed from gray and dark blue respectively, to white in order to avoid abuse scenarios.

Tibia Website Changes

  • The background was changed from the Zao theme artwork to another artwork featuring the revamped Colossus , people riding Mounts and somebody casting one of the new lightning spells (see it here ).

Changes in the Tibia Rules

The Tibia Rules have been reformed within the update. Below there is a list with the major changes to the rules:

  • You are allowed to speak in any language in the game and on the boards, with the exception of the Auditorium board, the Event board and the Proposal board.
  • In the game and on the boards, the posting of links to other websites are basically allowed, given that the links do not violate another Tibia Rule (for example, you will be able to send links of your YouTube video or your facebook profile).
  • Rule 3b concerning the abuse of game weaknesses has been removed. Thus, teleporting and multi-clienting by using multiple computers without manipulating the client is not treated as a rule violation anymore.
