Summer Updates

Summary

A new continent was added: Oramond , with many new creatures, items and quest lines.

See also: Updates Help

Update Teasers

  1. Land, Ho!
  2. Mooh'nsters
  3. Come To Rathleton!
  4. Return To Sender
  5. Improved Animations

Changes

  • The continent of Oramond was implemented, with its city Rathleton
  • Glooth Engineer Outfits were added.
  • Many animations were improved.
  • A new bonus system for shared experience:
    • Shared experience: 20% (for all combinations) plus
    • 2 different vocations 10%
    • 3 different vocations 40%
    • 4 different vocations 80%
  • Varying Monster Spawns

New Locations

  • An island in the east called Oramond , with a town called Rathleton and new hunting places.
    • Oramond/Southern Plains
    • Oramond/Eastern Plains
    • Oramond/North-Eastern Plains
    • Oramond/Western Plains
    • Oramond/Peppermoon Bells Cave
    • Oramond/New Sewers
    • Oramond/Abandoned Sewers
