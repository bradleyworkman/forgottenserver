Summary

Various fixes were introduced, including many Dawnport issues.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

Dawnport Issues

  • Once you are level 20 you cannot leave the temple upstairs anymore.
  • The Orc Berserker which sometimes spawned on the last floor of the Orc Cave was removed.
  • It is not possible anymore to transfer items from Dawnport to Rookgaard .
  • The vocation equipment of the vocations chests cannot be thrown back to Dawnport any longer.
  • Garamond no longer sells Energy Wave nor does he offer Summon Spells .
  • PvP is not possible anymore inside the Adventurer's Outpost .

Animation and Graphic Issues

  • Missing frames were added to the animation of the Invisible spell.
  • Items now sink if they are thrown onto Lava .
  • The lava riddle in the Pits of Inferno works as intended again.
  • Pythius the Rotten is reachable again in battle.
  • The speed of the animations in the Stand-Alone Client was adjusted.
  • Small issues with the female Conjurer Outfit and the Kingly Deer mount were fixed.
  • Some minor graphic bugs were fixed.

Miscellaneous Changes

  • Players cannot leave the Island of Destiny with the wrong level anymore.
  • Emma can now give you permission to obtain the Nature Magic Spellbook again if you have lost it.
  • Captain Tiberius on Travora now always transports players back to the city where they originally came from.
  • NPC Rabaz and raid boss Fernfang cannot be blocked anymore.
  • Free Account players are no longer allowed to enter a Fury Gate .
  • An issue that caused a crash of the Tibia Flash Client (for example due to closing Backpacks very fast) was fixed.
  • Unjustified points are displayed correctly now.
  • The Animate Dead Rune and Convince Creature Rune can no longer be used on other players.
  • Using the above-mentioned runes and the Disintegrate Rune does not count as an aggressive action anymore.
  • Magic Walls now work correctly on Hardcore PvP worlds.
  • Some map bugs and typos were fixed.
  • Characters of level 1 and 2 will benefit from a bonus that doubles their experience gain, this bonus will slowly decrease as they advance in level until it reaches the normal experience gain at level 50.
  • Graphical fixes of Beastmaster Outfits and Champion Outfits .
  • An issue with NPC Chemar was fixed.
