Major Updates

Summary

Introduced the Guilds System , vocation promotions and Gamemasters

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Guilds can now be created and joined to on Tibia.com
  • Vocation Promotions were introduced, you can now become an Elite Knight , Elder Druid , Royal Paladin or Master Sorcerer from King Tibianus or Queen Eloise .
  • New spells were introduced.
  • The Creature AI was improved.
  • Gamemasters were introduced.
