Summary

The creatures on the "top 20 hunting grounds" have been adjusted and their experience and/or loot decreased. Creatures and hunting grounds from the Summer Update 2019 and Winter Update 2019 have also been adjusted.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Several creatures had their experience decreased (see table for details).
  • Several creatures had their loot decreased (see table for details).
  • The Kilmaresh Central Steppe , Kilmaresh Southern Steppe and Green Belt in Kilmaresh are now more populated.
  • More creatures now roam the Netherworld , Zarganash and Barren Drift .
  • Barren Drift's Evil Prospectors raid now triggers every 30 minutes automatically if player involvement does not meet the requirements.
  • Parts of Kilmaresh Central Steppe have also been made more accessible in order to make hunting easier.

There were eight batches of changes in this version. Two days after the client patch was released, CipSoft updated some of the creatures again. The main objective of this second batch was to change the focus of the Ferumbras' Ascension Quest areas from Gold to Experience by increasing their experience to about 80% of the pre-balance values but significantly reducing their loot (35%-60%) instead. Five days later, the third batch decreased the experience True Asuri while increased the loot of some other monsters. Three days later, a fourth batch was launched. Five days later, the fifth batch was launched. Six days later, the sixth batch was launched. Seventeen days later, the seventh batch was launched. Eighteen days later, the eighth batch was launched. A few months later, the ninth batch was launched, and on September 01 a tenth batch was launched, which included creatures from the recently released Summer Update.
