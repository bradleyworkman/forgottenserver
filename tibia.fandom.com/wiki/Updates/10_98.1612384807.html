Updates/10.98

Minor patch 10.98 was released on September 13, 2016 , together with the official release of Tibia 11 . From now on, players will receive the following message in red every 30 minutes: The Tibia client you are currently using will soon no longer be supported. You can already download the new Tibia client 11 from www.tibia.com. In addition, red messages are not displayed in the game window anymore.
