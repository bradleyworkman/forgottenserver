Updates/10.75

Minor patch 10.75 was released on February 10, 2015 , and mainly served to fix both graphic and map bugs, together with some typos and NPC dialogues .

Further Changes

  • The Ceremonial Garb Outfits were added to the client data, and made available to buy on tibia.com two days later, on February 12, 2015 .
  • The proper nouns in the items' names Orshabaal's Brain and Morgaroth's Heart were capitalized.
  • TibiaBrasileiros ' fansite item was implemented, the Assassin Doll .
