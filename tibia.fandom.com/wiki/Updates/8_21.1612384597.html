Summary

Released shortly after the major update, contains some bugfixes and improvements based on feedback gathered.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Some respawns were revised and reduced in order to balance their difficulty.
  • The loot rate of Wyrms and Sea Serpents was revised.
  • Changes to Earth Elementals and Quara Hydromancers difficulty.
  • Fixes to NPCs Raffael , Janz , Nah'Bob , Lynda and Angelina .

Alterations in creatures, drops and respawns

  • Respawn of cockroach in the island of the novices reduced;
  • Loot rate for wyrms and Sea Serpents improved;
  • Loot rate of elemental soils in the elemental spheres improved;
  • Massive Earth Elemental removed from the Edron Bog Raider Cave ;
  • Grim Reapers removed from deeper Ankrahmun Tombs ;
  • Number of Earth Elementals in their cave in Edron reduced;
  • Earth Elementals difficulty level decreased;
  • Quara Hydromancers can not summon Water Elementals any more.

Changes in the NPCs

  • Raffael now sells arrows for the correct price;
  • Janz came back to sell hoists statues;
  • Nah'Bob also purchase magic versions of Spike Sword and War Hammer ;
  • Error corrected in quest for mage addon, with Lynda or Angelina .

Other corrections

  • Anti-luring system around Venore Amazon Camp and the room of The Count was improved;
  • Many bugs in the map were fixed.
Beyond the cited ones above, other small bugs had been corrected throughout the 3 days of patch.
