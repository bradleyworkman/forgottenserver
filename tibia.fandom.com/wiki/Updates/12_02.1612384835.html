Summary

Introduced many general fixes as well as adding the content for the Winterlight Solstice event to the game.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • An issue causing some players to disconnect from the game when they open their Battle Stats in the Character Information has been resolved.
  • Lumbering Carnivor now spawns with full health points.
  • Winterblade and Summerblade are now tradeable via the Market .
  • Dream Essence Egg as well as several rubbish items can now be traded via the Market and stowed into the Supply Stash .
  • Changed key number for Buried Cathedral wooden key.
  • From now on, elemental shrines on Feyrist teleport players back to Thais when they have used the winter and the summer court's portal in order to get there.
  • Players can now walk over the Store decoration Festive Filled Shoes .
  • Efreet , Marid , Blue Djinn and Green Djinn are no longer attacking other monsters.
  • Gloombringer , Spawn of Despair , Eclipse Knight , Harbringer of Darkness and Essence of Darkness entries were removed from the Bestiary again.
  • Added message to Faceless Bane boss battle in case the room is already occupied.
  • A protection zone field was removed from the Path of Defiances dungeon.
  • Several minor map and NPC fixes.
  • The items, outfits and creatures related to the Winterlight Solstice event were added to the client.
Announced simply as another round of "Bugfixes and Content Changes", this update also had the introduction of the Winterlight Solstice event data to the client, which was previously announced as part of the Winter Update 2018 but was not actually implemented with its release.
