Summary

Introduced the link copy protection

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • When a link is copied from the client, a warning is shown about the hacking risks of external links.

In this patch CipSoft " enhanced the security measures concerning copying and pasting links ingame ". When you select a link and copy it a warning will appear saying:

Warning!
You are about to copy a link. Keep in mind that unofficial websites may be used to hack your account! Continue?

However, some problems occurred with the patch and therefore it was only online for a short time. The following problems are known:

  • Kicks
  • Graphical cloning
Several days after the bug CipSoft announced those who not only died due to the bug but also sent a bug/crash report would be compensated with 7 days premium time . The problems were fixed in a patch the same day: Updates/8.52 .
