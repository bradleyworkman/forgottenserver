Changes

  • Rise of Devovorga :
    • Once stage 2 is completed, stage 3 will be initiated sometime between 8 and 14 hours after the next server save .
    • In order to get the Devovorga's Nemesis achievement , you now only need to significantly contribute in the final fight with Devovorga . You do not need to be the player dealing the most damage anymore.
  • Their Master's Voice :
    • Slime Gobblers can now be used every 5 seconds instead of every 15.
    • The delay between the golem waves has been removed.
    • The amount of golem waves was decreased from 25 to 20.
  • Moohtant Cudgel - its shielding modifier was removed and its defence value was raised by 1.
  • Forbidden Fruit Task - if you lost any required item, you can reset the task by saying 'collect' to Chartan again.
  • The Black Knight Doll , official fansite item of TibiaLatina.wikia.com , was implemented.
  • Some erroneous or incomplete NPC transcripts were adjusted.
  • Some map bugs, typos and minor graphical glitches were fixed.

Its testing stage was officially announced here .

Proposed Party Spell Changes

In the testing phase announcement, the following changes were proposed for Party Spells but were not implemented :

  • Heal Party will be replaced by the spell "Restore Party". This spell is a stronger version of "Mass Healing" and will restore health points of the spell caster and all party members within a radius of 4 squares. It has a cooldown of 2 minutes.
  • Train Party will be replaced by the spell "Shield Party". This spell will raise the shielding skill of the spell caster and all party members within a radius of 4 squares by 10% for 2 minutes. It has a cooldown of 2 seconds.
  • Protect Party will be replaced by the spell "Boost Party". This spell will raise the main vocation skill of the spell caster and each party member within a radius of 4 squares by 10% for 2 minutes. Main vocation skills are: Axe, club, and sword fighting for knights. Distance fighting for paladins and magic level for druids and sorcerers. It has a cooldown of 2 seconds.
  • Enchant Party will be replaced by the spell "Recharge Party". This spell will restore mana of all party members within a radius of 4 squares. It is similar to the druid's "Restore Party" spell with the notable difference that it does not affect the spell caster. It has a cooldown of 2 minutes.
