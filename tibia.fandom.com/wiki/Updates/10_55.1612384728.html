Summary

The island of Dawnport was introduced for starting players, among other smaller changes

See also: Updates Help

Update Teasers

  1. Let's Test a New Dawn... .

Changes

  • The power of skills was slightly adjusted.
  • New characters now start with 55 Mana Points instead of just 5.
  • The Adventurer's Blessing was introduced.
  • In addition to the new animations implemented in Update 10.51 , several more were introduced, which can be seen here .
  • The possibility for hotkey presets was implemented for the Stand-Alone Client .
  • The whole new island of Dawnport was introduced, replacing Rookgaard as beginner's island.
