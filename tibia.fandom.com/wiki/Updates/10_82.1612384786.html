Updates/10.82

Minor patch 10.82 was released on September 30, 2015 , to implement some content fixes and changes.

Changes

  • The ability to buy Blessings was added to the Store along with the ability to buy Temple Teleports .
  • Some changes were made to Dawnport and its tutorial to further optimise both.
