Summary

Fixed an issue with Mad Sheeps .

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • A character will only be able to summon a mad sheep about twice a day.
  • Characters in a protection zone will be able to remove a mad sheep by using one of the following items on it: Shovel , Rope , Light Shovel , Elvenhair Rope , Whacking Driller of Fate , Squeezing Gear of Girlpower or Sneaky Stabber of Eliteness .
