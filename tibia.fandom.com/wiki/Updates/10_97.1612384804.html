Updates/10.97

Minor patch 10.97 was released on Aug 30, 2016 to implement some fixes and changes and included a Store update as well.

Changes

  • Jorge has expanded his stock of replica items. The following fansite replicas can now be exchanged for Silver Raid Tokens :
    • Medusa Skull (Replica)
    • Golden Newspaper (Replica)
    • Bag of Oriental Spices (Replica)
    • Imortus (Replica)
    • Encyclopedia (Replica)
    • Doll of Durin The Almighty (Replica)
    • Hand Puppets (Replica)
    • Music Box (Replica)
  • The Rift Warrior Outfits can only be worn by Premium Accounts
  • The security warning for the following links will no longer be displayed in forum posts:
    • the trace routes trace-eu.tibia.com and trace-usa.tibia.com
    • links to facebook.com
    • links to youtube.com
    • links to Tibia's test website test.tibia.com
  • An issue with the animation of certain objects in the Stand-Alone Client where the first frame of an animation was skipped has been fixed. The Tibia Flash Client already displayed the correct animation of such objects.
  • A bunch of map bugs, graphical bugs, typos and other minor text issues were fixed.

Store Products

The three products mentioned in this news post are introduced to the Store .

  • XP Boost
  • Gold Pouch
  • Gold Converter

Furthermore some new decorations and new store categories are introduced. These can be seen in the items and objects sections of this page.
