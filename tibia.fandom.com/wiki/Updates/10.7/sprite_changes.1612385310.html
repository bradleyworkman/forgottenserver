This is a list of Items , Objects and Outfits which had their sprite changed during Updates/10.7 .

Summary

The sprite changes feature 1 improved creature animation and 9 new creature sprites. Also, some items or objects have a reduction in the number of frames, because in the past duplicate frames were allowed, but they are now compressed to show one frame for a longer time period instead of repeating the same frame several times.
