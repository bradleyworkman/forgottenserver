Summary

The magic system was revamped, adding wands and rods, faster mana regeneration and more, as well as graphical improvements.

See also: Updates Help

Update Teasers

  1. Update Teaser and New Supported Fansite
  2. Final Update Teaser and New Supported Fansite

Changes

  • The sprites of coastlines and mountains were improved.
  • Wands and Rods were introduced, magical weapons for sorcerers and druids respectively.
  • Mana regeneration was increased, but also the mana cost of spell was increased. However, instant spells will be cheaper.
  • A level requirement was introduced to spells.
  • Coloured light sources were added.
  • Writable documents now show a label indicating who was the last person to edit it and the date.
  • Deeper Banuta was added.

Wands and Rod

Wands and Rods were introduced to aid sorcerers and druids in their lower levels. While being wielded, they attack the targeted enemy from distance, consuming mana with each shot. The mana used costs towards magic level progress.

Spells Adjustments

This update replaced the former magic level requirement that spells had in favour of a experience level requirement.

Since the mana regeneration speed was adjusted, the mana required to cast most of the spells was increased by the same factor. This means that the time needed to regenerate the mana needed for a specific spell was not increased. In some cases, the time was reduced, as some instant spells remained unchanged.
