Major Updates

Summary

This update introduced the Premium System , Edron and the Housing System .

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Players are now able to buy Premium Time to get extra benefits.
  • The island of Edron was added. The first premium-only area.
  • The first strike spells were added.
  • Houses were introduced to the game.
In addition to the game changes introduced by this update. The foundation of CipSoft GmbH as a company was announced.
