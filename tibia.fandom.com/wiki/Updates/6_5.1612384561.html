Major Updates

Summary

The second server Nova was introduced as well as the island of Cormaya and other areas.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • The world previously known as just "Tibia" was renamed Antica .
  • The new world Nova was created.
  • An account number and password were first required in order to login to the game.
  • The island of Cormaya was created with new NPCs and houses.
  • The Ice Islands and Fields of Glory were expanded with new quests.
