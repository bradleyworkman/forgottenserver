Summary

Implemented Golden Outfits and other improvements.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Implementation of Golden Outfits .
  • Rusty Armors , Rusty Legs , Rusty Helmets and Rusty Shields were renamed and each one of them has a unique sprite from now on.
  • Deaths in Duelling Arenas and Monster Arenas will no longer fill up health and mana. Characters that they on those arenas will respawn in the foyer with 40 hit points and the amount of mana it had upon death. Additionally, arena NPCs now offer Health Potions , Strong Health Potions , Great Health Potions and Ultimate Health Potions for a higher price.
  • Two new Loyalty Titles have been added: Savant of Tibia (6000 Loyalty Points) and Enlightened of Tibia (7000 Loyalty Points).
  • Future Store items were also implemented on this update.
