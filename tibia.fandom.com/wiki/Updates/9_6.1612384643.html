Changes

  • Training Schools were implemented for premium players to train offline.
  • New houses were added to the game around the Tibian lands. The houses were designed by players who has won the contest " Your Dream Home " hosted by CipSoft .
  • A system to prevent house trashing was implemented. You now need to be invited to the house to be able to throw items into it.
  • New Gnome areas were added.
    • Gnomegate
    • Crystal Grounds
    • Truffels Garden
    • Gnomebase Alpha
    • Mushroom Gardens
    • Hot Spot
    • Golem Workshop
    • Warzones
      • Warzone 1
      • Warzone 2
      • Warzone 3
  • A new way of traveling with Crystal Teleporters between some cities was implemented.
  • Two new outfits and four new mounts have been added.
  • Target Dummies were removed, and replaced with the opportunity to get a Gnarlhound mount .
  • Infernal Bolts gained 30 attack points, raised from attack 42 to attack 72. Additionally, Soul Orbs can now be forged into 3 Infernal Bolts (with a chance to get 6).
  • Loot was added to Massive Fire Elementals .
  • It is now easier to access Diseased Bill : a ramp descends directly to his dungeon, instead of having to clear the bog raider pit.

Tibia Website Changes

Offline Training

Training Schools opened up in all major cities in Tibia to give premium players an opportunity to advance the skills of their characters while being offline. You are now able to choose one of the following skill combinations for your training: Magic level + Shielding , Distance Fighting + Shielding, Sword Fighting + Shielding, Axe Fighting + Shielding, or Club Fighting + Shielding. You simply have to use the corresponding Skill Trainer in a training school to log out in order to activate offline training for your character.

Statistics have shown that it works at 50% of efficiency of the normal training. Besides, it seems that in all cases it's like training shielding with only one monster hitting, which means 25% of efficiency comparing to normal training. For instance, 12 hours of any melee+shielding offline training would mean 6 hours of melee and 3 hours of shielding online training.
