Patch Updates

Summary

This update introduced PvP adjustments tested in preview worlds , as well as other loot adjustments.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Loot of Demons and Hellhounds was adjusted.
  • Blocked  summons cannot be used any longer to trap people in a stack. The summons can now be killed by the trapped players.
  • An issue where a player could get a skull while defending himself in dove mode has been fixed as well.
  • Some minor technical PVP issues were addressed.
  • Some of the NPCs were edited concerning their  new icons .
  • Some map issues have been fixed.
  • The content for Chyllfroest Mini World Change was added to the game data. It was announced later on March 21st 2014.

Loot Adjustments

  • Demons now drop less Gold and more Platinum Coins and no longer carry Double Axes . They also drop more Demon Horns , Assassin Stars , Small Emeralds , Stealth Rings , Rings of Healing and Might Rings . The following items were added to its loot: Great Spirit Potion , Demonic Essence , Small Topaz , Small Ruby , Small Amethyst and Red Gem . ( Loot Statistics )
  • Hellhounds now carry a higher average of Platinum Coins and no longer drop Gold Coins , Great Health Potions and Spike Swords . The following items were added to its loot: Great Spirit Potion , Ultimate Health Potion , Blazing Bone , Fiery Heart , Small Ruby , Small Emerald , Small Topaz , Green Gem , Yellow Gem , Red Gem , Green Piece of Cloth , Yellow Piece of Cloth , Red Piece of Cloth , Hardened Bone , Amber Staff , Onyx Flail , Ruthless Axe , Magma Coat , Magma Boots and Magma Amulet . ( Loot Statistics )
  • (Not officially announced) Chakoya Tribewarden , Chakoya Toolshaper and maybe Chakoya Windcaller now drop Fireproof Horn .
