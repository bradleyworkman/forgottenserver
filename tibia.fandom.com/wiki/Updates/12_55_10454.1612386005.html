Summary

General changes regarding the recent Vocation Adjustments Update .

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Knights can now buy Strong Mana Kegs in the Store and use them, while paladins can buy Great Mana Kegs in the Store and use them.
  • Quivers can no longer be sold to NPCs .
  • Magic Shield is now given in absolute numbers in the Status Bar and no longer as a percentage.
  • The descriptions of Protector and Blood Rage in the spell library have been changed to reflect the latest changes.
