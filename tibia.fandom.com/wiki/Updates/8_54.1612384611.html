Summary

The new continent Zao was added, the introduction of most creature products and many new items and creatures.

See also: Updates Help

Update Teasers

  1. A New Horizon
  2. Untold Mysteries
  3. Terrifying Creatures
  4. A Precious Parcel

Changes

  • The continent of Zao was added, with its underground city Farmine and a new quest storyline.
  • New creatures such as High Class Lizard , Draken and more.
  • Various creatures drop more, lighter items, these are usually called creature products .
  • Some creatures drop another food instead of meat: Bandits drop Tomatoes , Smuggler - Raspberry , Wild Warriors - Eggs , Hydras - Cucumbers , Skunks - Bulb of Garlic , Badgers - Beetroots , Elf - Plums , Nomads - Potatoes .
  • Houses that have tiles along the water were given them back to use again.
  • Snap-backs are now only possible when stacking into other characters, creatures, or objects (See Fourth Update Teaser).
  • Lucius is visiting Kazordoon , near the entrance Ore Wagons, to provide rewards to Lightbearers .
  • Rotworm doesn't drop Legion Helmet anymore.

Tibia Website Changes

  • The background was changed from the Fafnar and Medusa artwork to a Zao theme with Ghastly Dragons , High Class Lizards , Warmaster Outfits , and an Orc Marauder (see it here ).

New Areas

The Update includes the city Farmine (dwarfish base) in the extended continent of Zao (west of Vengoth ) with the following areas:

  • Chazorai
  • Dragonblaze Peaks
  • Muggy Plains
  • Zao Mountain
  • Zao Steppe
  • Zzaion

Changes in Sprites

  • Cocktail Glass
  • Rust Remover
  • Egg of The Many
  • Rotten Heart of a Tree
  • Hat for Eclesius (Dark)
  • Hat for Eclesius (Pink)
  • Hat for Eclesius (Wooden)
  • Some jungle plants /trees.
