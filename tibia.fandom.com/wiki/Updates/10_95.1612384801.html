Updates/10.95

Minor patch 10.95 was released on Jun 14, 2016 to implement some fixes.

Changes

  • The entrance area of the Ankrahmun Monster Arena can no longer be entered with a Protection Zone Block .
  • The chance to get a Pit Demon Doll from a Surprise Cube has been increased.
  • The exit teleporters in the Heart of Destruction areas can now be used with a Protection Zone Block .
  • Also, if you exceed the time limit that applies in these areas, you are now kicked out to the correct place.
  • Unstable Sparks , as well as some other event monsters, underwent some adjustments.
  • The bosses Rupture , Eradicator , and Outburst can now be repeated after 20 hours.
  • You can no longer walk over the following Store furniture items: Predator Lamp , Lit Predator Lamp , Protectress Lamp , Lit Protectress Lamp , Baby Dragon , Hamster in a Wheel and Cat in a Basket .
  • Some minor issues such as typos were also fixed.
