Summary

The Flash Client ended its Beta stage and was officially launched as a legit alternative to the stand-alone client. A few changes were made to Rookgaard and the Island of Destiny .

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • Rat , Spider , Poison Spider and Bug had their loot changed
  • Players now only need to load 2 catapults instead of 4 in The Rookie Guard Quest 's second mission.
  • The four vocation teachers in Island of Destiny will do a quiz after asking them for detailed information.
  • NPC Weaponmaster will now give the weapon of choice for Knights . He will also explain the benefits and drawbacks of each weapon type.
  • Players may now hunt until up to level 10 on their vocation-specific dungeon in Island of Destiny .
  • The Armoured War Horse was introduced to the game, although it's been in the data since Update 9.1 .

Client Changes and New Features

It was also announced that the Flash Client had left its Beta stage. Link to the official News Article

  • It is now possible to store the VIP list and Flash client options on CipSoft 's servers. This allows players to use the same client settings on different computers.
  • The VIP list options of stand-alone client users are also stored on servers now.
  • A Migration Tool has been added in order to transfer old settings to the servers.
  • When multiple players are on a single tile ("stacking") you can now look at each of them instead of the player on top, using the Battle Window . When looking from within the game window, you (still) only look at the player on the top.
