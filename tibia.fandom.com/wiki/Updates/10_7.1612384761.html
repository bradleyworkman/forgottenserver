Updates/10.7

The 2014 Winter Update was released 10 December 2014 . Its version number is 10.7 .

Update Teasers

  1. The Challenger III
  2. Smoke Under Water
  3. Wanted for High Treason Against the City of Rathleton
  4. Guild Features

New Features and Changes

  • Additions to Oramond :
    • Rathleton Quest : a new rank ( Squire ) which opens up the new combat-heavy Hero of Rathleton Quest .
    • Several new missions added to the Oramond Quest .
  • New guild features .
  • The Tibia website background artwork was changed to a new one.
  • New item functions:
    • Cobra Tongue (used in the Lion's Rock Quest )
    • Small Amethyst (used in the Lion's Rock Quest )
    • Small Ruby (used in the Lion's Rock Quest )
    • Small Sapphire (used in the Lion's Rock Quest )
    • Small Topaz (used in the Lion's Rock Quest )
    • Honeycomb (used in The Cursed Crystal Quest )
    • Green Crystal Splinter (used in The Cursed Crystal Quest )
    • Flask of Embalming Fluid (used in The Cursed Crystal Quest )
    • Petrified Scream (used in The Cursed Crystal Quest )
    • Piece of Crocodile Leather (used in the Oramond Quest )
    • Shrimp (used in the Glooth Factory Quest )
    • Mind Stone (used in the Twenty Miles Beneath the Sea Quest )
    • Lyre (used in the Glooth Factory Quest )
    • Special Flask (Holy Water) (used in the Glooth Factory Quest )
  • The Bank in Venore has some water leakage ( Dropping Water ) in the south-eastern corner because there is a small lake on the floor above. Also the jail (room of Sylvester ).
  • Many sprite changes .

Guild Changes

  • New Guild features will be added:
    • Changes to the Guild Channel channel:
      • If a player has a guild, the guild chat will automatically open when the player logs in.
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be allowed to invite non-members into the channel. This will last until the non-member logs out.
      • The leader and all vice leaders will use orange font .
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be able to create a guild message; a message of up to 255 characters that is displayed to guild members when they log in ( not when they enter the channel).
    • Guild Events :
      • A new section called Guild Events added to guild pages on the Tibia website.
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be able to start guild events.
      • Guild events can be public or exclusive .
    • Guild Applications
      • The leaders and all vice leaders will be able to allow or deny guild recruitment applications.
      • If applications are allowed, non-members can apply to the guild and provide an introduction to themselves in writing.
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be able to accept or reject each application.
    • Guild Board
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be able to moderate their private guild board by closing, re-opening and deleting threads.
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be able to sticky threads. Only their own threads can be stickied and can be done at thread creation or by editing an existing thread.
    • Disbanding Notice
      • The leader and all vice leaders will receive a letter in their Inbox when their guild is close to disbanding.
    • Guild Leader Election
      • When a guild becomes leaderless, the vice leaders can elect a new member to become the leader.
    • Guild Information
      • The Tibia website will contain more information about guilds on their overview pages. Information includes: vocation, level, online status.
    • Guild Bank
      • Guilds will have a bank account to share money with the guild.
      • All members can deposit money in the guild bank account.
      • The leader and all vice leaders will be able to withdraw and transfer money from the guild bank.
      • The guild bank will have a log showing the activity of the bank in the last 30 days. It will be visible to all members.
      • Guild-related transactions will be debited to the guild bank account. To avoid accidental loss of guildhalls, if the guild bank has insufficient funds, the guild leader's bank account will be debited instead. The fund for guild wars must always come from the guild bank.
    • Guild Activity Log
      • The guild activity log will list all guild activity within the last 30 days such as invitations and players joined, as well as rank changes.
    • Guild Depot
      • Every guildhall in Tibia will have a Depot . This will come about a week after the game update (announced on Major Game Update 10.70 ).
