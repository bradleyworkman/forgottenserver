Summary

This update introduced the Browse Field feature.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • The Browse Field feature was implemented to prevent depot and corpse trashing. For more information, see the article.
  • The Houses and Guildhalls of players who are scheduled for deletion will automatically have all their invitation and subowner lists reseted.

Bugs and Implications

  • Using the browse field feature you can find, move and take items that are under immovable objects, like rocks.
  • Some quests may be "cheated" now, such as Draconia Quest , where quest objectives (e.g. daily spawned keys) lie under an elemental field.
  • The server no longer checks if an item's movement is valid. This is subject to exploitation with unofficial software if you consider that players can be pushed regardless of their position in a stack, or regardless of the items beneath them.
  • PvP players have debated whether the ability to loot through Magic Walls / Wild Growth / Fields is appropriate since players can act back in a PvP server.
