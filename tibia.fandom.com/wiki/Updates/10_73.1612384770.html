Updates/10.73

Minor patch 10.73 was released on Jan 20, 2015 .

Changes

  • The PvP Expert Mode in characters' combat control is not visible anymore on Hardcore and Retro Open PvP game worlds.
  • It's now again possible to put Premium Scrolls in Purses .
  • It is not possible anymore to heal players out of a Protection Zone who are unjustified attacking other players on Open PvP game worlds.
  • A small graphical issue with the Ranger Outfits has been fixed.
  • The raid bosses Minotaur Wallbreaker and Glooth Bomb cannot be blocked by players anymore.
  • Private guild events are now visible for all guild members.
  • It is not possible anymore to accept guild applications during a guild war.
  • An issue regarding depositing in and withdrawing from Guild Bank accounts has been fixed.
  • Players on Retro Open PvP worlds can now fight Killing in the Name of... Quest bosses only once every 22 hours.
  • Some graphic as well as map bugs were fixed, together with some typos and NPC dialogues .
    • 3 new Mounts : Emerald Waccoon , Night Waccoon and Ringtail Waccoon . They are made obtainable the next day, Jan 21, 2015 .
    • Heavily Bound Book now has 199 writeable characters instead of 99
    • Truelove Teddy , Crimson Rose and Sweetheart Ring are added, probably for Valentine's Day .
