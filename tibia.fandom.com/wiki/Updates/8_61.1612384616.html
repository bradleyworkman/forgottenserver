Summary

Gamemasters were removed from the game, Beholders were renamed to Bonelords and other minor changes.

See also: Updates Help

Update Teasers

  1. Upcoming Client Patch

Changes

  • Gamemasters were removed from the game.
  • The Gamemaster Board became the Help Board.
  • Jean Pierre now gives the correct amount of Demonic Candy Balls . Players affected by the patch can receive additional candy to compensate for the mission candy.
  • Client-side features (such as Ctrl + R) related to gamemasters were removed and the ability to report character names and comments on the website was introduced.
  • It is now possible to get His True Face after having already finished the Blood Brothers Quest .
  • Coolie Hat was renamed to Sedge Hat .
  • The Helmet of the Deep now works correctly on types of underwater wooden floor when under the effect of Coconut Shrimp Bake .
  • The Sweet Mangonaise Elixir now also works when wearing a Death Ring .
  • Beholders had their name changed to Bonelords , as well as all references to this race (books, creature products etc.)
  • Water Elementals now have corpses and also received a major loot change.
  • Braindeaths can now cross magic fields.
  • The achievement Turncoat gives now 4 points instead of just 3.
Even though it wasn't announced as part of the patch, the content for Rise of Devovorga was implemented in this patch. Details were later announced in a featured article .
