Major Updates

Summary

This updated introduced the continent of Darama , and other areas were revised.

See also: Updates Help

Changes

  • The continent of Darama (sans Ankrahmun ) was introduced.
    • A new hometown Darashia , with new houses.
    • New areas:
      • Dark Pyramid
      • Drefia
      • Darashia Rotworm Caves
      • Ghostship
  • Updates to Kazordoon
    • Mad Mage Valley updated
      • Dwarf Mines were expanded outside of Kazordoon
      • Kazordoon Minotaurs new area
      • Paradox Tower new area
  • Updates to Plains of Havoc
    • New Cyclops cave with quest
    • Minotaurs added
This update did not have an official release number but occurred between updates 6.61 and 6.97. The only announcement ahead of time was on June 05, 2002 .
