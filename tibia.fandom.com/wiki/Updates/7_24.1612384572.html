Minor Updates

Summary

The party system and automatic bans for excessive player killing were introduced, among other changes.

See also: Updates Help

Update Teasers

  1. Test Server for Minor Update

Changes

  • A new party system was introduced, to replace the old duel mode ( green skulls ).
  • Characters who reach twice the amount of unjustified kills that are needed to get a red skull, will be banished or deleted automatically.
  • New houses and quests were implemented.
