Since it was introduced in 1997, Tibia has undergone several changes. Each of these updates brings improvements to the game, such as better graphics, new Items , new Creatures , and new or changed abilities.

Why do some of the updates listed not have version numbers? According to CipSoft GmbH Customer Service, version numbers are only given if the client is changed and a new client download is required. Any updates that can be done at the server alone do not get version numbers.

Update 7.5, August 9, 2005 (Summer Update 2005)

The huge jungle area known as Tiquanda and its main city of Port Hope are introduced for Premium account players. The city is surrounded by settlements of dangerous monsters, including Banuta , Chor and Trapwood . Mighty Hydras roam parts of the jungle, waiting to take explorers unaware. Free Account players can explore the new and mysterious Dark Cathedral near Plains of Havoc , where Bandits , Assassins , Dark Monks and more are waiting for them. Worms are introduced with this update; you must have some in your inventory in order to fish. Soul Points are also introduced with this update, to help control cheating. Other improvements include Furniture that you buy in parcels (no more kicking large tables up the streets!) and the Anti- Luring feature that causes monsters pulled too far from their spawn point to vanish. Spears "break" and disappear with this update; however, to help compensate, Small Stones , Throwing Knives and Throwing Stars can be reused (they used to vanish immediately upon use).

Update 7.6, December 12, 2005 (Christmas Update 2005)

This is a huge graphics update, with many of the graphics being refreshed and redone. Lighting effects are added. The Magic System is changed dramatically, with the rate of Mana regeneration speeding up 4x from before the update. The amount of mana required for spells is adjusted accordingly. The ability to cast Spells is changed from a Magic Level based requirement to a Level requirement. Wands and Rods are introduced for mages. All letters, tags and writable papers now show the name of the last character to edit them. The Linux client is no longer supported by Cip as of this update.

Update 11.40, July 25, 2017 (Summer Update 2017)

Implementation of the fairy-themed island, Feyrist , which is affected by the improved day-night cycle. The quests Cults of Tibia and Threatened Dreams as well as the Daily Reward System and the new analytics features ( Cyclopedia , Loot Analyser , Supply Analyser , Impact Analyser , XP Analyser , Hunting Session Analyser , Drop Tracker and Quest Tracker ) were also implemented with this update. The removal of Enchanting and Charging Weapon systems marked this update. All previously chargeable weapons received imbuement slots and had their stats improved, while most of the enchantable weapons got imbuement slots. Enchanted gems are now dropped by new monsters. Some spells were removed, added and revamped. One new special summon spell was implemented for each vocation. Five old paladin supply spells were removed while Conjure Diamond Arrow and Conjure Spectral Bolt spells were created. The Enchant Staff spell was replaced by Conjure Wand of Darkness . The mana cost of the Challenge spell was raised from 30 to 40. Additionally, challenged creatures now target caster for at least 6 seconds and do not run away during this time. The new game world type Retro Hardcore PvP was also implemented with this update, however the first 3 servers with this PvP type were implemented only two weeks later. Jewelry Shopkeeper NPCs now buy small enchanted gems. Gold Tokens can be used to purchase creature products needed for the vampirism, void and strike imbuements from Yana . Jorge has expanded his stock of fansite items replica by 4. And Cillia now sells replications of enchanted and charged weapons for 500 gp each.

Server Updates

CipSoft is updating their servers constantly. When server updates are applied, they usually do not require a new client version. Most bug fixes count as server updates; anything to do with maps, NPCs, quests, and creatures usually only require a server update. These updates are usually applied at server save.
